About

Why this exists

I built this for people like me — anyone trying to break out of the casual habit of using AI as an internet search. The goal is a foundational understanding of AI, AI fluency, AI ethics, and enough practical prompting to make something that doesn't exist in the world yet.

Everything here comes from my own learning: notes I wrote by hand, the exact videos I watched, and the exercises I worked through from scratch. I use Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, ChatGPT, and whatever else fits the task — because the training that got me here says to use every available tool and find the AI that excels at the job in front of you. I'd suggest you do the same.

It's free, and it stays current on purpose. The code is on GitHub if you want to see how it works.
